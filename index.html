Bio

The Pearls are a genre-bending group from Casper, Wyoming, pulling from a wide mix of influences. Their songs drift from indie to alternative rock, with sets that ride through love, sadness, passion, and pure energy — engaging for anyone and an all-around good time.

They have played all over Wyoming, from hometown rooms to stages that feel like the last warm night of summer.
